  • Welcome to English in a Minute!
  • Social means you like to be around people. A butterfly is an insect.
  • SOCIAL BUTTERFLY
  • So a social butterfly must be an insect that likes to fly around people?
  • So I went to a party last weekend and guess who was there? Jane from accounting.
  • Let me guess. She sat in a corner the whole time and didn't talk to anyone... like she does at work.
  • No, just the opposite! She was quite the social butterfly -- moving from one person to the next, laughing and talking all night long!
  • A social butterfly moves among people at a gathering easily, like a butterfly flying from flower to flower. A social butterfly enjoys interacting with people.
  • And that's English in a Minute.
