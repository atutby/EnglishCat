    «Газетная лексика» – Anonymous – Анонимный
    • Welcome to the Voice of America's News Words.
    • In September, The New York Times newspaper published an opinion criticizing U.S. President Donald Trump.
    • ANONYMOUS
    • The anonymous opinion was written by a senior official in Trump's government.
    • "When you tell me about some anonymous source within the administration, probably who's failing and probably here for all the wrong reasons..."
    • Someone who is anonymous is not identified by name. This opinion was unusual because major newspapers in the U.S. usually require writers to sign their work.
    • When an author does not want to be known, publishers call him or her "anonymous."
    «Газетная лексика» – Superfood – Суперпродукты
    • Welcome to the Voice of America's News Words.
    • The Baobab is a well-known tree in Africa.
    • SUPERFOOD
    • Inside the green shell that comes from the tree is a tasty fruit. For farmers in Senegal, the business of selling fruit from the baobab tree has increased in the last year. The fruit has become a popular superfood in the United States and Europe.
    • A superfood is something to eat that has a lot of nutrients and vitamins. Superfoods improve health and well-being.
    • They include salmon, sardines, spinach, broccoli, baobab and blueberries.
    • Now you know what superfood means.
    «Газетная лексика» – Incubator – Инкубатор
    • Welcome to the Voice of America's News Words.
    • Washing your hands is one of the easiest ways to stop the spread of disease.
    • INCUBATOR
    • Two women created a fun way for children to wash their hands. It is a cleaner drawn on the skin and removed by washing. The women did not have much business experience, so they turned to a business incubator for help in selling the product.
    • A business incubator is an organization that helps young companies grow and develop. It offers training, technical support, and other services.
    • More commonly, an incubator is a device that keeps eggs or even new babies warm.
